Indian Railway

Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a coastal city located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. The city is well connected to other parts of the country through railways, and has a railway station named "Visakhapatnam Junction" which is one of the busiest railway stations in the country. Several trains run to and from Visakhapatnam, connecting it to major cities like Chennai, Kolkata, Delhi and Mumbai, making it an important transport hub in the region.

Adilabad

Adilabad is a small town in the state of Telangana, India. The town has a railway station named "Adilabad Railway Station" which is located on the Secunderabad-Manmad section of the South Central Railway. The station is well-connected to nearby towns and cities like Hyderabad, Nizamabad and Karimnagar, making it an important transport hub in the region. Several express and passenger trains run through Adilabad Railway Station, providing easy access to different parts of the country.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is a major transport hub in South India. The city has three major railway stations - Hyderabad Deccan, Secunderabad Junction, and Kacheguda Railway Station - that connect it to different parts of the country. Many express, superfast, and local trains run to and from Hyderabad, providing easy access to nearby towns and cities like Vijayawada, Bangalore, Chennai, and Mumbai.
